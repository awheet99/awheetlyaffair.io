About Us

Taking baking classes at community college and cooking schools started a passion for baking. Sharing sweet treats with friends and family, my hobby continues to grow. A favorite memory is the thought of baked goods cooking on a kitchen window sill. Finding a beautiful kitchen on Park Street helped me decide that a walk-up window would be a perfect way to share these treats with others.

A Wheetly Affair is located at 2114 Park Street in the New Amsterdam neighborhood. Stop by the window and check the chalkboard for our daily menu of baked goods. We accept orders for your special events.

I look forward to spending time with you at A Wheetly Affair.
